Experience as an Exchange Student

In 2014, as I was seventeen years old, my adventure of living for a year in another country had begun. I had the opportunity to know the YFU International Organization and chose to study in another school with another family for a year in Germany. It was the best experience I've ever had. I've made a lot of friends there and I've got to learn their very interesting culture and language, of course. I went there having a First Certificate English degree and the first months I spoke only English, after the sixth month with a lot of hard work and listening I started to speak German and by the end I got to speak it fluently. Since then I have great interest for learning languages, like the computer program languages!
